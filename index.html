Sai Pallavi

Sai Pallavi is a popular South Indian actress and dancer known for her natural acting style and down-to-earth personality. She first gained fame with her role as Malar in the Malayalam film *Premam* (2015), followed by notable performances in *Fidaa* (2017), *Love Story* (2021), and *Virata Parvam* (2022). Known for her no-makeup look and grounded approach to her roles, she embraces simplicity and avoids endorsing skin-lightening products. Sai Pallavi’s dance skills and authentic portrayals have won her a dedicated fan base and critical acclaim in Telugu, Tamil, and Malayalam cinema.

Kajal Aggarwal

Kajal Aggarwal is a renowned Indian actress known for her roles in Tamil, Telugu, and Hindi films, with notable works like *Magadheera* and *Thuppakki*. She debuted in 2004 and gained fame for her versatile acting and charming screen presence. Kajal married Gautam Kitchlu in 2020 and continues to have a successful career in Indian cinema. She is also recognized for her strong social media presence and large fan following.
